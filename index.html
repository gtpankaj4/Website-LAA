About Us

Where Education Meets Innovation

Situated in Tyanglaphat, Kirtipur, Kathmandu, Lok Aadarsha Academy, established in 2052 BS, is dedicated to "Lead and achieve academic excellence."

We foster an engaging, student-centered learning environment, encouraging exploration and growth. Our facilities include spacious classrooms, playgrounds, a futsal court, a standard canteen, a hostel, a health care unit, an art and sculpture unit, yoga and auditorium halls, a basketball court, science labs, and CC cameras. Join us in our commitment to academic excellence and continuous improvement.

Why LAA?

Music Classes

We offer comprehensive music classes that help students explore their musical talents and learn various instruments.

Futsal Field

Our state-of-the-art futsal field provides students with the opportunity to engage in team sports and stay active.

Drawing Classes

Students can unleash their creativity through our engaging drawing classes, designed to develop artistic skills.

Extra-Curricular Activities (ECAs)

Our diverse range of ECAs ensures holistic development, offering students various clubs and activities beyond academics.

Educational Expeditions & Tours

Educational tours provide hands-on learning experiences, making education more interactive and enjoyable. Our educational expeditions encourage exploration and adventure, fostering a love for learning through real-world experiences.

Science Lab

Our well-equipped science lab gives students the chance to conduct experiments and deepen their understanding of scientific concepts.
